Who are we?

BNM STACK EXCHANGE is a student friendly project that all is open for all BNMIT students. This platform is built to enable students to form a community where they can share their difficulties and can get solutions from their peers or their faculty members.

What do we at BNM STACK EXCHANGE offer?

  • Ask questions and receive relevent answers from your peers and seniors
  • Keep a track of all the questions you have asked so far.
  • Show the code where you are facing problems.
